Services Our Dispatchers
Provide to You

✓ Load sourcing & rate negotiation

We know that generic load boards often overlook 26ft box trucks. Our dispatchers use specialized platforms and real-time market analytics to find high-paying, compatible loads that fit your truck's specifications. We negotiate the best rates so that every mile counts toward your profitability.

✓ Operational support

The Logity Dispatch systems account for every detail, from weight balancing for compliance to ensuring equipment requirements are met. We prioritize loads that match your truck's specs so you're always running efficiently and safely.

✓ Route planning & optimization

We design optimized routes for urban and regional deliveries, minimizing empty miles and maximizing your revenue per trip. Our intelligent backhaul matching significantly reduces deadhead miles for better profitability.

While we don't directly handle reporting or insurance, we connect you with trusted partners and guide you through compliance steps — eliminating operational headaches so you can focus on growing your business.
